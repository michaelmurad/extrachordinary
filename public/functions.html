Harmonic Functions

Harmonic Function is a title that represents the feeling (emotion) that certain chord transmits to the listener.

Tonic Function

Transmits a feeling of rest, stability and finalization. Promotes the idea of conclusion.

Dominant Function

Transmits instability and tension feeling. Promotes the idea of preparation for the tonic.

Pre-dominant

It is the middle ground between the two previous functions. We can say that it creates a preparation feeling, but with less intensity and may migrate either to the dominant function (intensifying the tension) or to the tonic (resting).
